About C13Features

What is C13Features?

Announced in 2020 in Variety , C13Features is the podcast movie division of award-winning podcast studio Cadence13. Each feature is a single, scripted, movie-length episode, with cinematic sound, top-level casting and a thrilling fictional story that takes the listener on a gripping audio experience. From the same studio that brings critically acclaimed and award-winning documentaries from C13Originals (Root of Evil, Gangster Capitalism, Gone South, Fallen Angel, Once Upon a Time… at Bennington College, It Was Said, Relative Unknown and more) comes a brand new listening experience with C13Features. From Treat, starring Kiernan Shipka, to Ghostwriter, starring Kate Mara and Adam Scott, the theater of the mind will be awakened.

What is a Podcast Movie?

A podcast movie is a feature length, on-demand audio only cinematic experience, designed specifically for your ears and imagination. Each story is developed with a beginning, a middle and an end, encompassing all that you expect from a movie, without the visuals. Combining the depth of characters and the arc of a brilliantly written, original story with high-level production quality, epic sound design, music, and top-level casting, each C13Features podcast movie will make the listener feel like they’re experiencing a blockbuster film, and transport them to these fictional worlds using only sound and imagination.
